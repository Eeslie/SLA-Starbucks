# How to Use the Self-Service Portal

Welcome to Starbucks Self-Service Portal! Find answers to common questions quickly and easily without waiting for support.

---

## What is the Self-Service Portal?

Our Self-Service Portal is a knowledge base of helpful articles and guides designed to help you resolve common issues independently. It's available 24/7 and contains step-by-step instructions for the most frequently asked questions.

---

## How to Access

1. **Go to Support Page**
   - Visit the Starbucks Support page
   - Look for the "Browse FAQs" button
   - Click to open the Self-Service Portal

2. **Search for Articles**
   - Use the search bar at the top
   - Type keywords related to your question
   - Browse results that match your search

3. **Filter by Category**
   - Click category buttons (All Topics, General, Orders, Rewards, Account, etc.)
   - Articles will filter to show only that category
   - Click "All Topics" to see everything again

---

## Finding the Right Article

### Using Search

- Type specific keywords (e.g., "password", "refund", "rewards")
- Search looks through article titles, content, and tags
- Results are sorted by helpfulness score (most helpful first)

### Using Categories

- **General**: General help topics and how-to guides
- **Orders**: Order issues, refunds, delivery problems
- **Rewards**: Rewards program, stars, benefits
- **Account**: Login, password, profile settings
- **App Support**: Mobile app issues and troubleshooting
- **Payment**: Payment methods and billing issues

---

## Reading Articles

1. **Click an Article**
   - Click on any article card to read the full content
   - Articles open in a detailed view with full formatting

2. **Navigate Between Articles**
   - Click "‚Üê Back to articles" to return to the list
   - Click another article to read it
   - Use the search bar to find related articles

3. **Provide Feedback**
   - After reading, click "Yes, helpful" or "Not helpful"
   - Your feedback helps improve articles for others
   - You can only provide feedback once per article

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific in Search**
   - Use specific keywords instead of general terms
   - Try different phrasings if you don't find results

2. **Check Multiple Articles**
   - Related articles might have additional information
   - Don't stop at the first result if it doesn't fully answer your question

3. **Read Carefully**
   - Articles often have step-by-step instructions
   - Follow each step in order for best results

---

## Still Need Help?

If you can't find an answer in the Self-Service Portal:

1. **Create a Support Ticket**
   - Click "Create Support Ticket" button at the bottom of any article
   - Provide details about your issue
   - Our team will respond within the SLA timeframe

2. **Check Your Ticket Status**
   - Use "Check Ticket Status" on the support page
   - Enter your ticket ID to see updates

---

## Need More Help?

If you're still having trouble finding what you need:

- Try different search terms
- Browse different categories
- Contact our support team for personalized assistance
```

```markdown
# Navigating the Support Website - Complete Guide

Learn how to navigate the Starbucks Support website and get the help you need quickly and efficiently.

---

## Getting Started

When you visit the Starbucks Support website, you'll see several options to help you:

1. **Self-Service Portal** - Browse helpful articles
2. **Live Chat** - Talk to our support assistant
3. **Ticket Status Check** - Check your existing ticket
4. **Create Support Ticket** - Request personalized help

---

## Self-Service Portal

### What Can You Find Here?

The Self-Service Portal contains articles on:

- **Account Management**: Password resets, profile updates, account settings
- **Order Help**: Refunds, order issues, delivery problems
- **Rewards Program**: Stars, benefits, tier information
- **App Support**: Mobile app troubleshooting and fixes
- **Payment Issues**: Billing, refunds, payment methods

### How to Use It

1. **Click "Browse FAQs"** button on the support page
2. **Search** for your topic or **browse by category**
3. **Click an article** to read the full guide
4. **Provide feedback** if the article was helpful
5. **Create a ticket** if you still need help

---

## Live Chat Support

### Features

- **Instant Responses**: Get immediate help from our assistant
- **Ticket Creation**: Create a support ticket directly from chat
- **SLA Tracking**: See your ticket's SLA status in real-time
- **Chat History**: View previous conversations

### How to Use

1. **Start Chat**: Click on the chat widget (bottom right)
2. **Describe Your Issue**: Type your question or problem
3. **Follow Prompts**: Answer questions to help the assistant understand
4. **Get Help**: Receive immediate answers or create a ticket
5. **Track SLA**: See how much time is left before response deadline

---

## Creating a Support Ticket

### When to Create a Ticket

Create a support ticket when:

- Self-service articles don't solve your issue
- You need personalized assistance
- You have a complex problem that needs investigation
- You need to follow up on a previous issue

### How to Create a Ticket

1. **Go to Support Page** or use the chat
2. **Describe Your Issue**: Provide clear details about the problem
3. **Provide Your Email**: Enter your email address
4. **Submit**: Your ticket will be created with automatic priority detection
5. **Save Ticket ID**: You'll receive a ticket ID to track status

### Ticket Priority

Tickets are automatically assigned priority based on:

- **Urgent**: Critical issues (security, payment, account compromised)
- **High**: Important issues (refunds, orders, login problems)
- **Medium**: General questions and assistance needed
- **Low**: Simple inquiries and feedback

---

## Checking Ticket Status

### How to Check

1. **Click "Check Ticket Status"** on the support page
2. **Enter Your Ticket ID**: Paste or type your ticket ID
3. **View Details**: See current status, updates, and chat history
4. **Resume Conversation**: Click "View Conversation" to continue chatting

### Ticket Statuses

- **Open**: Ticket is new and waiting for agent response
- **In Progress**: An agent is actively working on your ticket
- **Resolved**: Issue has been fixed
- **Closed**: Ticket is completed

---

## Website Features

### Search Functionality

- **Article Search**: Find articles by keywords
- **Category Filtering**: Filter articles by topic
- **Real-time Results**: See results as you type

### Responsive Design

- **Mobile Friendly**: Works on phones and tablets
- **Desktop Optimized**: Great experience on computers
- **Easy Navigation**: Simple, intuitive interface

### Helpful Tools

- **Copy Ticket ID**: Click to copy your ticket ID
- **Article Feedback**: Help us improve articles
- **Quick Links**: Access common topics instantly

---

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Try Self-Service First**: Often faster than waiting for support
2. **Be Specific**: Provide detailed information when creating tickets
3. **Check Your Email**: Important updates are sent via email
4. **Track Your Ticket**: Monitor progress using ticket status
5. **Save Your Ticket ID**: You'll need it to check status

---

## Getting the Most Help

### Before Creating a Ticket

1. Search the Self-Service Portal
2. Check if your issue is covered in articles
3. Try suggested solutions from articles
4. Note any error messages or details

### When Creating a Ticket

1. Be clear and specific about the problem
2. Include relevant details (dates, order numbers, etc.)
3. Attach screenshots if helpful
4. Mention what you've already tried

---

## Common Questions

### How long does it take to get a response?

Response time depends on your ticket priority:
- **Urgent**: Within 1 hour
- **High**: Within 4 hours
- **Medium**: Within 24 hours
- **Low**: Within 48 hours

### Can I edit my ticket after creating it?

Yes, you can update your ticket through the chat or by creating a follow-up message.

### How do I know if my ticket was received?

You'll receive a confirmation with your ticket ID immediately after creation.

---

## Need More Help?

If you're having trouble navigating the website:

- Check the Self-Service Portal for more guides
- Use the chat feature to ask questions
- Create a support ticket for personalized assistance
- Contact us through other channels if needed
```
