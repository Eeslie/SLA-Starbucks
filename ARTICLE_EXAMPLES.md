## Example Article 2: Account Management

**Title:** How to Reset Your Starbucks Account Password

**Category:** Account

**Content:**
```markdown
# How to Reset Your Starbucks Account Password

Forgot your password? Don't worry! You can easily reset it in just a few steps.

## Steps to Reset Password

1. **Go to Starbucks Login Page**
   - Visit the Starbucks website or open the mobile app
   - Click on "Sign In"

2. **Click "Forgot Password"**
   - Below the password field, you'll see a "Forgot Password?" link
   - Click on it to start the reset process

3. **Enter Your Email**
   - Type the email address associated with your Starbucks account
   - Click "Send Reset Link"

4. **Check Your Email**
   - Open your email inbox
   - Look for an email from Starbucks (check spam folder if needed)
   - Click the password reset link in the email

5. **Create New Password**
   - Enter your new password (must be at least 8 characters)
   - Confirm your new password
   - Click "Reset Password"

## Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- Must include at least one letter and one number
- Cannot be the same as your last 3 passwords

## Still Having Issues?

If you don't receive the reset email:
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Wait 5-10 minutes and try again
- Contact support if the problem persists
```

## Example Article 3: Orders & Refunds

**Title:** How to Request a Refund for Mobile Orders

**Category:** Orders

**Content:**
```markdown
# How to Request a Refund for Mobile Orders

Need a refund for a mobile order? Here's how to get it processed quickly.

## When Can You Request a Refund?

You can request a refund if:
- Your order was incorrect or missing items
- You received the wrong order
- The order was charged but never received
- You were charged multiple times

## How to Request a Refund

### Option 1: Through the App
1. Open the Starbucks mobile app
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Find the order you want refunded
4. Tap "Get Help" or "Report Issue"
5. Select "Request Refund"
6. Provide details about the issue
7. Submit your request

### Option 2: Contact Support
1. Visit our support page
2. Create a support ticket
3. Include:
   - Order number
   - Date and time of order
   - Description of the issue
   - Receipt or order confirmation

## Refund Processing Time

- **Credit/Debit Cards**: 5-10 business days
- **Starbucks Card**: Usually instant, up to 24 hours
- **PayPal**: 3-5 business days

## Important Notes

- Refunds must be requested within 30 days of purchase
- Keep your order confirmation number handy
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method
```

## Example Article 5: App Support

**Title:** Troubleshooting Mobile App Login Issues

**Category:** App Support

**Content:**
```markdown
# Troubleshooting Mobile App Login Issues

Having trouble logging into the Starbucks mobile app? Try these solutions.

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue: "Invalid Email or Password"

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Your Credentials**
   - Make sure you're using the correct email address
   - Verify your password (check for caps lock)
   - Try typing your password in a text editor first to see it

2. **Reset Your Password**
   - Use the "Forgot Password" link
   - Follow the email instructions
   - Create a new password

3. **Clear App Cache**
   - iOS: Delete and reinstall the app
   - Android: Go to Settings > Apps > Starbucks > Clear Cache

### Issue: App Crashes on Login

**Solutions**:
1. **Update the App**
   - Check App Store (iOS) or Play Store (Android)
   - Update to the latest version

2. **Restart Your Device**
   - Turn off and on your phone
   - Try logging in again

3. **Reinstall the App**
   - Delete the app completely
   - Download and install fresh from the store

### Issue: "Account Locked"

**Solutions**:
1. **Wait 30 Minutes**
   - Accounts are temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts
   - Wait 30 minutes and try again

2. **Reset Password**
   - Use the password reset feature
   - This will unlock your account

3. **Contact Support**
   - If still locked after 30 minutes, contact support

## Still Need Help?

If none of these solutions work:
- Create a support ticket with details about the issue
- Include your device type (iOS/Android) and app version
- Describe exactly what happens when you try to log in
```
